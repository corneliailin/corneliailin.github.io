Cornelia Ilin

I am a Faculty Associate at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I work on research topics related to Health Economics and IO.

Before joining UW, I was an Associate Economist at Analysis Group, Menlo Park - CA office.

I received my Ph.D. in Applied Economics from the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My advisors were Guanming Shi, Daniel Phaneuf and Jean Paul Chavas.
